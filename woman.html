    • WomanInc

    The current WomanInc website lacks hierarchy, making it hard for users to easily find information in a timely manner. For our project, we proposed to redesign the layout of key web pages based on findings through our user tests. We restructured the information architecture and hierarchy after rounds of card storting and design testing. The overall goal from our project was to achieve a design that would help women navigate smoothly through Womaninc.org with speed.

    *This was created as a class project.

    Overview

    Through our research, we found that users had difficulties acessing useful information due to the lack of hierachery and typographic differentiation throughout the website. We also concluded that there was a need for a better nagivation system so that users could effectiently address their needs. Our overall goal for this project was to provide clear objectives and options for users seeking help to accomplish their objectives in a timelier fashion.

    User
    Research

    During our user interviews, we discovered that users have difficulties quickly accessing information due to the lack of typographic hierarchy and organization of information. Therefore, we believe that by reorganizing the display of information, with an emphasis on hierarchy, the user will accomplish more in a shorter period of time which is important given their potential predicament.

    Ideation

    To properly prioritize the most important features, we created an affinity map and conducted multiple rounds of card sorting. These card sorting rounds helped us decide how the navigation bar should be set and how we should categorize the sections based on importance. As we did these card sorting exercies, we also referenced other similar websites to make sure that we were heading towards the right direction. Along with looking at other websites, we also conducted a heuristic evaluation for the orginial website to explore the positive and negative factors of the webpage. During our ideation phase, we also created a mood board to help us decide on our color palette. We wanted to create a color scheme that was inviting and made users feel at ease.

    Prototyping

    From our user tests, we had the following takeaways:
    -Exaggerate the icons/ add more resources
    -Shorten meter on text
    -Centered circles while hovering

    The Final

    In our future iterations and/or opportunities, we would like to add links to more resources that could benefit the user.
